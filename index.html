The challenge

Construction generates over 50% of the UK’s landfill, contributes to more than 40% of the UK’s carbon footprint, and has a successful reclaim rate of less than 2%.

By digitising the secondary market in construction materials we will close the loop between demolition and construction. This will radically lower waste and our collective carbon footprint.

Our platform

We connect together yards, demolition sites and contractors to help our industry partners to source and sell secondary construction components. Our platform allows partners to access complimentary services of pre-demolition auditing, environmental analysis and BIM model generation, to remove the pain points around specifying and understanding secondary materials.

Become a partner

Digital Tools

Our app and reclaim search engine removes the friction from supplying and sourcing secondary materials. As a partner you can easily upload information at the pre- demolition, demolition or material storage stage, and our search engine enables architects and contractors to search for suitable materials.

We also offer the technology to create 3D digital models of reclaim components so they can be used in BIM software, and a digital passporting and cloud service of existing materials so they can be re-used at a future date.

Consultancy

We can advise on making your construction project more circular. Every site has its own constraints and possibilities, and we bring a data-first and creative approach to closing the loop, and enabling your project to save carbon and money at both the demolition and construction phase.
