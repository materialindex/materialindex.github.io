Material Audits

We provide material audits at any stage of a bulding's lifecycle. We can provide valuation of the materials in a building prior to it undergoing deconstruction, and tell you the potential embodied carbon saving of these materials being re-used.

Material Brokerage

We can assist you with the sourcing and specification of secondary materials for your next project. This will enable you to meet your embodied carbon targets.
